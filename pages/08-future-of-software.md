# Build vs Reuse — A New Calculus

<div class="mt-8 text-lg">

The decision to write your own vs reuse may come down to:

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-6 mt-6">
  <div class="p-4 border-2 border-teal-200 rounded-lg">
    <p class="font-bold accent">Token cost</p>
    <p class="text-sm opacity-60 mt-1">How much does it cost the agent to understand, modify, and maintain?</p>
  </div>
  <div class="p-4 border-2 border-teal-200 rounded-lg">
    <p class="font-bold accent">Complexity surface</p>
    <p class="text-sm opacity-60 mt-1">A simpler codebase = cheaper for agents to work with</p>
  </div>
  <div class="p-4 border-2 border-teal-200 rounded-lg">
    <p class="font-bold accent">Control</p>
    <p class="text-sm opacity-60 mt-1">Your fork, your rules. No upstream surprises.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<p class="mt-6 opacity-50">This is why understanding the architecture matters — so you can make an informed fork</p>
